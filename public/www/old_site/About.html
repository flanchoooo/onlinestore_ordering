WildTech Solutions was founded in 2008 with the strategic decision to focus its attention on Medical Retail Technical Service Provision incorporating the setting up of a hardware and software distribution.


WildTech Solutions selects best of breed medical systems for all major medical business verticals and has skilled up on these products to ensure localized service provision in order to reduce the total cost of ownership of IT systems as

well as reduce the barriers of entry into retail automation.


Key to providing a solution to our customers is providing a software solution that is matched to their type of business. Around this we build the capacity to procure and deliver quality hardware at competitive prices, Retail peripherals are specialized and we have reduced the time to automate stores down to a few days, without in any way disrupting trading.

With over 50 retail customers through the channel, and 50 to 60 non-retail customers, the development of a 365-day Call Centre to manage quick resolution of issues has allowed the improvement of customer service.
