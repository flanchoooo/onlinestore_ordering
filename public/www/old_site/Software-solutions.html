OUR PRODUCTS


ProPharm

Propharm is a system written specifically for the pharmacy retail market with emphasis on combined drug dispensing and over the counter sales. Through years of development this package has been designed around sound pharmacy concepts, giving you all the information and data needed to make informed critical decisions and to run a successful pharmacy business. Industry leading, standard features include an unlimited number of stock items and accounts in multiple formats or types for both Debtors and Creditors. Combined with a full reporting system, various automation functions and users gain maximum usability. System also offers incredible security features which include Level access passwords that can be user defined, user name and password authentication tool that is highly secure and efficient for per user reconciliation and audit purposes


Complienz

Complienz is a drug interaction software program, including information such as side-effects, mode of action, warnings and special notes. There are also drug-to-food interactions and the effect of smoking, caffeine and alternative drugs on prescribed drugs, but, we also equip the user with the knowledge of why and when it will have an effect. It also includes information such as label directions, side-effects, mode of action, warnings and special notes at the click of a button. Complienz explains the mechanism behind drug-to-drug interactions, so that it can make sense and you can form your own opinions and make your own decisions.

Counseling pamphlets can be printed for the patient. It is extremely easy to print since it is part of the software. It is written in layman’s English. It contains all the drug warnings and food-interactions and the effect of smoking on drugs – all the things you are supposed to warn your patients about. It also tells the patient what to do when he should e.g. stop smoking. Better patient compliance will follow.


Health Care Payment and Remittance Advice Electronic Billing system (Switching) – Medical clearing house

We are in the process of building a switch to offer this unique service, the automated Electronic Remittance Advice system, which supplies medical scheme remittances electronically. This allows pharmacies/practitioners to reconcile their medical scheme claims within seconds!

Electronic remittance advice helps save time by automatically posting insurance payments based on electronic reports delivered to practices. Users can receive electronic remittance advice (ERA) reports, receive a receipt of an electronic funds transfer (EFT), and use electronic remittance advice reports to automatically post insurance payments and reconcile balances. By receiving electronic payments and automating the payment posting process, medical institutions get paid faster and save time
